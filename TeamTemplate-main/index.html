Top Three Reasons We Picked Our Topic

  • (1) Music is a universal language for those who appreciate it, and for that I respect it.
  • (2) Music has the ability to almost freeze time and provide an escape to outterworldly and external negativites
  • (3) Music can bring one back to times and places of youth, linking them to past selves and identities.
